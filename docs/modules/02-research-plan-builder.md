# Module 02: Research Plan Builder

## Description

The Research Plan Builder takes a target person and a research objective and generates a structured, GPS-compliant research plan. The module uses the Research Agent Assignment v2.1 prompt engine to produce prioritized lists of source types to search, record repositories to check, and specific strategies tailored to the time period, geography, and ethnic or religious community of the subject. Plans are stored in Supabase and linked to the person record. Each plan tracks its own progress as items are completed through the Research Log and Research To-Do Tracker.
